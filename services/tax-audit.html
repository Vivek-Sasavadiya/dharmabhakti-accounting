Who Needs a Tax Audit?

Businesses

If total sales/turnover exceeds ₹1 Crore in a financial year.

Professionals

If gross receipts exceed ₹50 Lakh (e.g., doctors, consultants, freelancers).

Presumptive Taxpayers

If you declare profits below 6–8% (Section 44AD/44ADA) and income exceeds basic exemption.

Loss Claims

If claiming business loss and not filing under presumptive taxation.
